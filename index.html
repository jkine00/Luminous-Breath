Privacy First

Luminous Breath does not collect, share, or sell any personal data.

All sessions, settings, and activity data are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to external servers.

If you choose to enable optional analytics or crash reporting, only anonymous diagnostic data (such as error logs and performance metrics) may be shared with Firebase to help improve app stability. No information that identifies you or your device is ever collected.

You can disable analytics or crash reporting at any time in the app settings. Tap the lock or unlock button on the Sessions View.
