Safety Notice

Breathing exercises with breath holds can affect oxygen and carbon dioxide levels and may cause lightheadedness or dizziness.

Always practice sitting or lying down in a safe place. Never use while driving, in water, or in any situation where fainting could cause harm, or without appropriate supervision.
